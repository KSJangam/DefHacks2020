Facts and Misconceptions about COVID-19

Possible Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Shortness of breath
  • Coughing
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Muscle aches
  • Headaches
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion, Runny nose
  • Other symptoms are possible*

People At Risk

  • The immunocompromised
  • Seniors
  • Those with Asthma
  • Those with HIV
  • Those with Liver or Lung problems

Preventive Measures

  • Self Isolation/Quarantine
  • Only leave home for essentials
  • Practice social distancing of at least 6 ft.
  • Wear a mask and gloves

Misconceptions

  • COVID-19 is like the flu
  • Consuming disinfectants can kill the virus
  • Antibiotics help against the coronavirus
  • You don't have the coronavirus if you can breath properly
Information learned from the CDC
