What is COVID-19?

A coronavirus is an upper respiratory infection. COVID-19, short for "coronavirus disease 2019," is the official name given by the World Health Organization to the disease caused by this newly identified coronavirus. COVID-19 has spread across the world, causing countries to close their borders and lock down.

How am I affected?

COVID-19 is a global pandemic. Unless you live in space, there is a high probability people in your country are infected. COVID-19 is spread very easily, and severe cases of COVID-19 can result in death.

The purpose of this website

is to help educate you on this pandemic and provide information so that you stay safe and healthy.
