Are you stuck at home during quarantine wondering what to do? Here are a few ideas of what to do with your extra free time.

Want to stay in shape but your local gym is closed due to quarantine? Here are some things you can do at home to stay fit.

Get a jump rope! Jump ropes are a cheap and effective tool you can use for cardio if you do not have a treadmill. In addition, push-ups, sit-ups, and squats require no equipment. If those exercises aren't enough for you, you can search up workout regimens that require no equipment! You can also search up diets to supplement your exercise.

Pick up new hobbies.

Now that you have a ton of extra free time, you can use it to learn a skill or a hobby you never had time for before! Be it cooking, coding, or even juggling; anything you want to learn you now can. The internet is an excellent resource for picking up new skills, so don't be afraid of trying out new things!

Do what you love.

Go on social media. Watch something with your family. Play video games. Read a book. Watch videos. Make you're own! During these times, keep your morale high by doing what you love doing. Just do it at home and stay safe!
