Why wear a mask?

It is imperative to wear a mask when leaving home. COVID-19 is spread mostly through respiratory fluids, which means coughing, sneezing, and even talking spread the disease. By wearing a mask, you are reducing the spread of the diease.

What if I am not sick?

Even if you do not show any symptoms, you should still wear a mask when entering a public space. You may be asymptomatic. Understand that symptoms of COVID-19 start to appear 2-14 days after being infected. Even if you test negative for the disease, you should wear a mask so other people will follow suit. Let's work together to reduce COVID-19 cases!

Where can I get a mask?

You can make your own mask if you want! You do not need anything fancy, just something to cover your face. If you do not want to make a mask, you can buy cheap ones from your local CVS or on Amazon. You can also order custom masks online if you are feeling fancy.
